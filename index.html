The Brain Health Blueprint

A One-Hour Physician Consultation

Dementia, and even cognitive decline, are not caused by one thing — and they cannot be addressed by one thing. They are the result of many risk factors accumulating quietly over decades. The Brain Health Blueprint is a one-hour physician consultation designed to do something no routine medical appointment has time for: build a complete, scored profile of your personal dementia and cognitive decline risk factors — across all six of our research-backed pillars — and hand you a structured, individualized plan to begin addressing them one by one.

The Memory Care Navigator

An Ongoing Monthly Concierge Program

Knowing your risk factors is the first step. What happens next — month after month, in a structured and physician-guided way — is what actually moves the needle. The Memory Care Navigator is a monthly concierge program built around one core idea: that defeating cognitive decline requires a plan that evolves with you.

What's Included Each Month

  • ◆A personalized monthly action plan with a focused risk factor target — one meaningful step at a time
  • ◆Longitudinal cognitive assessment trending — so you can see your trajectory and results over time
  • ◆Access to a proprietary cognitive sharpening app, selected and monitored as part of your plan
  • ◆A patient diary framework — because accountability between sessions is where real progress is made

How It Works

1
Start with the BlueprintYour initial consultation becomes the foundation that every monthly session builds upon.
2
Monthly SessionsWe meet — in person or virtually — review your progress, update your risk factor profile, and set a focused intention for the weeks that lie ahead.
3
Track Your TrajectoryCognitive assessments and diary entries build a longitudinal picture of your brain health that deepens with every session.
4
Cumulative ProgressSmall, structured victories compound over time — and you are never navigating this journey alone.
